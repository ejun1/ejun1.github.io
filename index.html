In California
A grunge typeface reflects the raw beauty and challenges of California's kelp forests, symbolizing their resilience amid rising temperatures and pollution. Its rugged texture captures the urgency and imperfect state of these ecosystems, raising awareness of conservation.
The overgrowth of kelp, particularly species of brown algae, along the California coast has been driven by a combination of climate change, rising ocean temperatures, and disrupted marine ecosystems. This overgrowth can have significant negative impacts on the ecosystem.
